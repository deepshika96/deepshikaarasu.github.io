--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About "Deepshika" in Sanskrit translates to "the light of a flame from a lamp", it signifies the presence of light as knowledge and truly; I derive the greatest joy in pursuing knowledge, analyzing and discussing it. I am passionate about biology and harnessing engineering technology to implement solutions in healthcare. Broadly, I am interested in science, technology, policy and how these aspects synergitically impact the real world. I have had the opportunity to travel around the world during the course of my training, which informs my interest in understanding cultures. I am also the COO of My Story As A (Wo)man, an NGO that addresses trauma faced by womxn in India. If not discussing science or feminism, you can find me sampling different cuisines, watercolour painting or reading the latest Goodread release.
